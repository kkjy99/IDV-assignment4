Singapore's Population Chloropleth (2024)

Background

The map below illustrates the population count at the subzone level in Singapore using a choropleth map. The darker the colour, the higher the population count. Subzones with zero population counts are shaded in grey.

Hover over each subzone for the name of the subzone, corresponding planning area and region and the population count.

Datasets used for above visualization retrieved from: Resident Population 2024 and Master Plan 2019 Subzone Boundary.

Data retrieved from data.gov.sg and singstat.gov.sg .
